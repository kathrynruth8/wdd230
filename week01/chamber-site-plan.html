Overview

Site Name

Anoka Chamber of Commerce. This name represents a city in which the chamber operates, Anoka, Minnesota.

Site Purpose

The Anoka Chamber of Commerce was established in 1952 and is still going strong. The vision of the commerce is to provide businesses resources and opportunities to help them grow, advocate with the government, education, and citizens to build a better community. They serve the cities of Andover, Anoka, Champlin, Coon Rapids, Dayton, Nowthen, Oak Grove, Ramsey, and St. Francis.

Home page

Welcome to MN Nice Cream

We are a Minnesota Ice Cream Shop located in Stillwater and Minneapolis Minnesota. Our goal is to serve up yummy Minnesota themed ice cream and bring smiles to your face. We are proud Minnesotans who love ice cream and all things MN Nice. Enjoy a sample, or five, and get yourself a scoop of our naturally flavored ice cream topped with our iconic edible glitter.

Our ice cream flavors are all original creations named after iconic Minnesota places, sayings, and landmarks.

Our Story

WHO WE ARE

We are lovers of Minnesota, ice cream, and all things fun! We hope to share that love with you through our tasty ice creams, and brightly colored shops.

ABOUT OUR FLAVORS

We value freshness at MN Nice Cream, so our ice creams are made of all natural ingredients. Our Minnesota themed flavors change each month to keep things fun and fresh. Come into our shop to vote on which flavors we need to bring back next month!

MEET KATE

MN Nice Founder Kate is ice cream's #1 fan. Her motto is "The best time for ice cream is always." She is also a proud Minnesotan. Kate loves all things fun and colorful.
